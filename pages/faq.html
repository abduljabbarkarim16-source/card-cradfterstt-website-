Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about streaming, gift cards, bill payments, and shopping services.

How do I add items to the cart?

Navigate to the service category, pick a service, customize your plan, then click "Add to Cart" in the panel.

Can I mix multiple services in one cart?

Yes. Add multiple items from different categories; the cart preview drawer shows all selected items and totals.

Do you support other streaming platforms?

Yes. We currently offer Netflix, Disney+, Prime Video, and HBO Max, and we can also help with other platforms on request.

How is the shipping & customs estimate calculated for shopping?

Item amount of USD is converted 7:1 to TTD, then customer rate at 8.5:1. Customs depends on category; the selected destination can remove customs for customer-owned skybox.

Where can I get support?

Use the Contact page or reach out using the number in the footer if you need help with an order or service.
